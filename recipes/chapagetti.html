Chapagetti

Description

This dish is considered an instant dish, being able to purchase this at a local convenience store. Although cheap, if done right, can taste just as good as a homemade dish.

Ingredients

  • Chapagetti package
  • Cucumber
  • Sesame seeds
  • Pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles and vegetable flakes for 3-4 minutes in boiling water
  2. Strain the noodles from the water
  3. Stir fry the noodles, powder packet, and vegetable flakes on a pan
  4. Put pepper flakes into the mixture once finished stir frying
  5. Once done, cut a cucumber into thin, vertical slices
  6. Put the chapagetti into a bowl and slices of cucumber on top
  7. Spray sesame seeds on top and enjoy!
