Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This Korean dish contains the iconic Korean side dish, Kimchi. By adding an egg on top, it helps create a cohesive balance with the spice from the Kimchi.

Ingredients

  • Calrose rice
  • Kimchi
  • Bacon
  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a rice cooker
  2. Stir fry the Kimchi along with the green onion and bacon in a wok
  3. Once the rice is done, add the rice into the wok and stir fry everything together
  4. Make an egg sunny side up
  5. Place the food in the wok in a bowl and put the egg on top
  6. Put some sesame seeds on top and enjoy your food!
